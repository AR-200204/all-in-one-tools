How to Calculate Hourly Wage

The standard formula for converting salary to an hourly rate is simple: Divide your annual salary by the total number of working hours in a year.

Standard Year: Most full-time jobs assume 40 hours per week for 52 weeks a year, totaling 2,080 hours.

For example, if you make $52,000 a year: $52,000 / 2,080 = $25.00 per hour.
